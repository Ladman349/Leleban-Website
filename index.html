The Story Behind Le Laban

From Humble Beginnings to Sweet Landings

Started by 4 college friends  ·  Dessert dreamers  ·  Franchise enquiries welcome

Le Laban began with four college friends, a lot of late-night conversations, and one shared obsession: building a dessert spot people would crave after dinner, after movies, after everything. What started small grew one spoonful at a time.

"From four college friends to Chennai dessert favourite."

From humble beginnings to four buzzing branches in Chennai, Le Laban is built around late-night dessert runs, post-dinner cravings, and the simple joy of sharing something sweet. We are also open for franchise and business enquiries on +91 85905 82635.
